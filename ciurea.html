Ciurea commune

The Ciurea commune is situated in the south of Iași, being one of the county's largest communes.

According to the National Institute of Statistics, Ciurea's population was 10954 in 2002, and 12604 in 2012. 87,6% of Ciurea's population were of Romanian ethnicity and 6,1% of were Romani according to the Housing and Population census in 2011.
