Thoughts

  • Smooth scrolling on the Mac

    I have been using a Mac for the last couple of years, using its trackpad or the Apple Magic Mouse. Recently, I switched to a Logitech MX Master 2S. I notice a big difference in the scrolling experience between Apple and non-Apple peripherals. It's like I went from a round-wheeled bicycle to a square-wheeled one. Things like these make Apple products feel like magic and are a differentiating factor for me. Pavel Fatin seems to know more about this(I have not yet read the entire article myself).

  • Everything sounds cooler in English than in Romanian

    Take my hometown for example, Lunca Cetățuii- Meadow Fortress, or Ion Fieraru- John Smith. I'm not sure whether this is a generally observed phenomena among Romanians.
